How to start drama

  • Gray (Clueless): Have no idea what happened.
  • Red (Gossiping): Actively spreading it to their friends.
  • Blue (Over It): They got bored and moved on.

The Rules of Gossip

Juiciness (Spread Rate): How likely someone is to tell a clueless friend about the rumor.

Attention Span (Forget Rate): How quickly they get bored of the drama and stop talking about it.

Moved On (SIR Model): Once they get bored, they are totally over it. They never spread it again.

Goldfish Memory (SIS Model): They forget the rumor, but they are ready to be completely shocked by the exact same thing again!
