How to use the Simulator

  • Gray (Susceptible): Hasn't heard the rumor yet.
  • Red (Infected): Actively spreading the rumor.
  • Blue (Recovered): Bored of the rumor and stopped spreading it.

The Math Models

Spread Rate (β): The probability a spreader tells a susceptible friend on each tick.

Forget Rate (γ): The probability a spreader gets bored and stops spreading.

SIR Model: People turn Blue and never spread it again.

SIS Model: People forget and turn Gray, ready to hear it again.
